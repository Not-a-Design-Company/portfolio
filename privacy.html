Privacy Policy

About

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and process the personal data you provide to us through our contact form . Please read this information carefully to understand how we treat your data.

Privacy

The personal data we collect through our contact form includes:
- Name and surname
- Email address
- Message sent!

The personal data provided will be used exclusively for the following purposes:
- Respond to your requests or questions submitted through the contact form.
- Provide information about our products/services, if requested.
- Maintain a log of communications for customer support and documentation purposes.

Your personal data will be kept for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes for which they were collected, unless you explicitly request their cancellation.

To ensure efficient processing of contact forms on our site, we use the third-party service FormSubmit . This service assists us in the collection and management of information shared through contact forms . However, it is important to note that our commitment to your privacy remains unchanged.
The personal data provided through the contact forms will not be sold, rented or shared with third parties, except in cases where this is required by law or is necessary to fulfill the purposes indicated above. FormSubmit acts as our data processor and operates under strict guidelines to ensure data security and confidentiality.
Information shared via FormSubmit is also subject to their privacy policy. To understand how FormSubmit collects, uses, and protects data, please review their Privacy Policy . This document provides details on the data processing by FormSubmit and on the measures adopted to guarantee the privacy and security of the information.
Our priority is your privacy, and we carefully use services like FormSubmit to ensure that your information is treated responsibly and in compliance with privacy regulations. If you have any questions or concerns about data processing or any other matter related to your privacy, please contact us using the information provided on our site.

We will take appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

As a Hosting service, we rely on Aruba for the infrastructure and operation of our site.
The data collected through the use of the site are necessary to fulfill Aruba's operational requirements. This data may include, but is not limited to, IP addresses, technical information and login data.
Aruba adopts security and data protection measures to ensure that information is stored in Italy and treated in accordance with applicable privacy laws. It is important to note that no one, with the exception of Not a Design Company, has direct access to the data collected through the site.
If you would like more details on how Aruba processes data and protects user privacy, please visit the privacy information pages provided by Aruba, such as " Aruba GDPR " and " Aruba Privacy Policy."

Legal

The processing of your personal data takes place on the basis of your explicit consent provided through the contact form.

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request the rectification or updating of your inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
- Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
- Revoke consent to the processing of your personal data.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be published on our site and we invite you to consult it periodically.

To exercise your privacy rights or for any questions regarding this Policy, please contact us at privacy@notadesigncompany.com.
